About Us

Obaida Ashfaque

With over the years of experience in the water purification industry, Obaida Ashfaque founded BLUVERA with a vision to provide the purest drinking water to every household and business in Banaras and beyond.

His commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made BLUVERA one of the most trusted names in packaged drinking water. Under his leadership, the company has grown from a small startup to serving thousands of customers across the region.

Obaida personally oversees every aspect of the business, from water sourcing to quality control, ensuring that every bottle of BLUVERA water meets the highest standards of purity and safety.
